Ideas

One of my strongest design points are ideas

I love working with ideas and coming up with concepts. That has always been the driving force within my design work.

I believe there is always a story behind every good idea and I enjoy creating narratives. I also enjoy incorporating humour and working with puns.

This concept for United Colors of Benneton takes the well known saying of 'what matters is on the inside', which I then applied within the context of fashion. I was exploring the idea of something more spiritual, that looks within the internal beauty, being applied to something more superficial, like fashion, that focuses upon the external beauty. With this juxtaposition I aimed to question these two ideals. United Colors of Benetton is well known for their controverial campaigns and thus with this idea I aimed to carry on in the same vein of thought.

The poster above was an idea for a pun. I like working with humour, whenever it can be incorporated.
