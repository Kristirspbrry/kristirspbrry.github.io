Quidco

I worked with Quidco to improve their Android and iPhone mobile apps. Their initial design was very similar, however after some UX research I introduced a more native design to both apps to ease the user experience, while still keeping the Quidco branding.

While tackling the mobile apps I also stepped in as Head of Design for a year for the team, leading 3 other designers and working together with lots of other teams in the company.

During my time at Quidco, I iniated several UX research exercises with helped immensely with the development of the Quidco brand.

What I did:
Art Direction
Dashboard Design
iOS design
Android design
Marketing Design

Besides working on mobile design, I redesigned the user's dashboard, following my UX research and testing.

Having branched out my design infuence across several fields in the company I also started working closely with Marketing and Acquisition teams on designing the cashback business by introducing more UX in the brand. It's something I really enjoy working with and will write more about in my blog.
