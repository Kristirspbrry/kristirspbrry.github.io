User centered re-design

Our objective of this project was to focus on user research and user journeys and using our data and research to re-design the Android and iOS apps.

The core team for this project included a PM and developers. Also, working with our data team, we gathered data from the data team and our user tests to inform our ideation phase.

We approached this project with the double diamond framework. The first step for us was to understand our users and how they shop before I could move on working on ideas for the business.

Working closely with the team we examined the data we had on user journeys and what we as a business aimed to achieve with our users to remap some of our flows and structure.

We also tackled some of the issues with UI. I backed up my reasoning with research and another case study. We aimed to improve the product UX and usability with this re-design as well as questioning how we could develop this product further to our users' needs.

We made some prototypes we could test some of the more complex UI solutions.

Since we have built native first experiences for both apps, the design system did differ a little bit, however, they both have common elements running throughout to ensure consistency of the brand.

I also have a few more case studies related to this project, but they were also a wider research study for Quidco.
