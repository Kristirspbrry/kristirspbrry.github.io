Hello there

I'm Kristina, a digital product designer and a self-taught developer living in the sunny ol' England. I love the countryside and writing about design. You can find both on my Instagram and my Blog.

I believe in good design, whichever aspect of that discipline it may be. I like to keep learning and so my projects range from digital publishing and branding to web and app design. Design is so much more than making something look pretty, design is business and that's what I'm all about.
