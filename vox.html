Brand Identity

Web design and branding

For this project I was asked to come up with a solution to a problem. This brand has a seprate webpage for each country they represent. They wanted the header of the site to be the image of the city that is being represented. However, the problem they faced was that each picture seemed different. As they didn't commission specialist photography, the photos used for each header of the city were from different sources, causing irregular looks throughout the sites.

My solution to this was using the same colours and graphically altering the images to make them appear uniform throughout the webpages.

I worked with the company's branding colours to be incorporated throughout the brand. As the brand's main focus is on images, this made it future proof as well, as now any image can be made to look on brand with this styling guide.
