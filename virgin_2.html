Virgin Sports

Digital / Wireframing / UX / Print / Layout / Editorial

Redwood Marketing Agency in London asked me to work on their Virgin Sports account to create digital and print visuals.

This was a very borad and all rounded role. I worked primarily with digital design, collaborating with other memebers of the team in making the Virgin website responsive across desktop, tablet and mobile. I worked on designing the layout for desktop and mobile views. I designed a number of wireframes to come up with the best solutions, while closely following Virgin's design guidelines.

I also worked with the print team on designing an A3 booklet. This was made working with InDesign, again following Virgin's brand guidelines and making sure the pages looked uniform throughout. For that we had a set of templates that we designed and referenced throughout.

One thing that I certainly had to learn quick was compound shapes in Photoshop. I have to admit I hadn't come across this until I had to work with Virgin's email designs. It made everything so much easier by dividing the content into blocks so editing the artwork was a breeze! I use this technique in my work a lot now too.

And last but not least, I made some quick illustrations for infographics. The one above was about racing. Funnily enough, I'm the least into sports but I learned a lot on this job! Not just about Formula One, but tennis, cricket, you name it!
