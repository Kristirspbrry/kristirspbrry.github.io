Digital Publishing

Working with layout, typography and art direction

As a senior a designer on this project, I was working on 10+ puclications. Initially, I helped convert old format Flash files written in Actionscript to the current day iPad to reach more users. I mainly worked on building templates and style guides, as well as working on the UX and user interface.

Our aim was to design these publications to be interactive and animated. To make it easier for the user to understand how it all works, we borrowed a lot of ideas from way the web fucntions, so it could be more relatable to what is already out there and a habit to the user.

The varying titles of the magazines required a different sort of art direction. One of my key focus was to deliver a design that reflected the content accordingly.

Every editorial piece was different, and depending on the content, it's design and layout varied. The one above was for Factor publication.
