Virgin Sports

Digital and Print design

I contracted for Redwood Marketing Agency in London, working on the Virgin Sports Campaigns.

This was a very borad and all rounded role. I worked primarily with digital design, collaborating with other memebers of the team in making the Virgin website responsive across desktop, tablet and mobile. This mainly involved designing the layout and deciding how it should change and reduce for mobile viewers. I designed a number of wireframes to come up with the best solutions, while closely following Virgin's design guidelines.

I also worked with the print team on designing an A3 booklet. This was made working with InDesign, again following Virgin's brand guidelines and making sure the pages looked uniform throughout. For that we had a set of templates that we designed and referenced throughout.

One other aspect of this job was working with emails as well as designing graphics. The emails followed a strict template and the key focus here was on detail - making sure everything is in the right place, order and following the correct design structure.

These are just a few examples of the sort of work I did while I was at Redwood. It was an all rounded role and a great challenge.
